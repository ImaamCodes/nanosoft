Housework Assistants

Nanosoft makes excellent machines to help with house chores like dishwashers, cooking bots, vacuum cleaners, laundry helpers.

ATVs, SUVs, Sedans, Commercial Buses, Hatchbacks, Commercial Trucks

Nanosoft makes fantastic ATVs like the Nanosoft 4Racer Terrain which has bulldozer wheels and an extra escape wheel to help get out of tricky situations.

Moreover, Nanosoft makes fantabulous SUVs like the Nanosoft Cruise4All which is an eleven-seater smart self-driving car equipped with the Nanosoft SDMC system.

Also, Nanosoft makes extraordinary sedans like the Nanosoft Leefy which is a five-seater that is able to steer smoothly without sudden jerks.

Additionally, Nanosoft makes marvellous commercial buses like the Nanosoft Probus NS920 which is a 40-seater double-decker bus equipped with smart door sytems and extra safety shields.

Furthermore, Nanosoft makes sleek hatchbacks like the Nanosoft XM220 hatchback equipped with an advanced fog light and the Nanosoft AWImComfy which allows it to adjust to the most extreme weather conditions.

In addition to this, Nanosoft makes powerful commercial trucks like the Nanosoft Delivery4Me which has an incredible storage space of 3500m3 plus an extra towing hatch for towing up to 3,000kg of cargo.
